Governance

The organization has adopted a governance structure similar to the Apache Foundation known for its ability to scale and foster a collaborative meritocracy. Specifically, there are the following units:

OS Planning Board
  • business affairs
  • legal affairs
Project Committees
  • where most work is done



Funding?

Right now this is a completely volunteer and personal-donation-based effort. Into the future, it is anticipated that background organizational costs (i.e. web hosting) will be covered by donations and that the bulk of the work at the project-level will be funded via individual agencies, research institutions or corporations.
